About

I'm Jason Rappaport, a product designer and entrepreneur in the greater Philadelphia region. I've been starting companies, building communities, and shipping software since I was 15. I graduated from Lehigh University in 2012 with a degree in product design, but spent most of my time working on my own companies. Since then, I've raised millions in venture capital for my businesses from investors like Josh Kopelman and the city of Philadelphia.

Check out my companies and products if you haven't: GoldenChaos-BTT, Zelda Universe, Zelda Maps, and Zelda Wiki. Got a cool project you'd like me to work on or take a look at? Contact me and let's chat.
