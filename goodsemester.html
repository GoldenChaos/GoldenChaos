GoodSemester was a truly modern learning management system for colleges and universities. It contained a full3suite of tools for online education including an interactive grade book, collaborative note taking tools, discussion boards, groupwork, assignment submission and more. GoodSemester entered public beta in early 2012. After discovering that users primarily wanted tutorial creation tools, the service was shut down to pivot into Squareknot.
